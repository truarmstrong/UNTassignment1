POPULATION ON THE RISE  

It's clear to say that North Texas will soon rise in this new decade. You might say that the 20s will be roaring more than the 1920s. All the businesses in major cities are relocating to Dallas-Fort Worth and other Texas cities. In 2019, the state's population was 29 million. It may increase up to 37 million or more by 2029.
