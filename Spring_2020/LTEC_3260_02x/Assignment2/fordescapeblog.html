Redesigned for 2020, the fourth-generation Ford Escape erases many deficiencies of its predecessor, a respectable model that aged well over seven model years. We’ve likened the new SUV’s rounded styling to that of the Porsche Macan, and it portends an equally well-rounded vehicle in many areas — but caveats apply.

The redesign offers turbocharged 1.5-liter and 2.0-liter engines, both with new eight-speed automatic transmissions, plus a hybrid and plug-in hybrid.
